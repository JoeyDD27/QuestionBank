## 概念部分

### Concept 1: LINEAR EQUATIONS
**原图**: `image483.png`

**提取内容**:
- 定义: A linear equation contains at least a variable which can only be raised to the power 1.
- 线性方程示例: $3x + 5 = 8$, $\frac{1}{2}x - 3 = 2$, $\frac{1+x}{3} = x$
- 非线性方程示例: $x^2 - 3x + 2 = 0$, $\frac{1}{x} = \sqrt{x}$, $x^3 = 8$

---

### Concept 2: SIDES OF AN EQUATION
**原图**: `image484.png`

**提取内容**:
- LHS (Left Hand Side): 等号左边
- RHS (Right Hand Side): 等号右边

---

### Concept 3: Solution
**原图**: `image485.png`

**提取内容**: The solution of a linear equation is the value of the variable which makes the equation true.

---

### Concept 4: ALGEBRAIC SOLUTION STEPS
**原图**: `image486.png`

**步骤**:
1. Decide how the expression containing the unknown has been 'built up'.
2. Perform inverse operations on both sides to 'undo' and isolate the unknown.
3. Check your solution by substitution.
